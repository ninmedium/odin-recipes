Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic Italian pasta dish, featuring long, thin noodles coated in a rich, savory tomato sauce. The sauce, often simmered with garlic, onions, and fresh herbs like basil, clings to each strand, creating a perfect balance of flavors in every bite. Finished with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese, itâ€™s a simple yet comforting meal that satisfies any craving.

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Tomato sauce
  • Parmesan cheese

Steps

  1. Cook spaghetti noodles in boiling, salted water until al dente, then drain.
  2. In a pan, heat tomato sauce with garlic and herbs, letting it simmer for a few minutes.
  3. Toss the cooked spaghetti in the sauce, then serve with a sprinkle of Parmesan cheese on top.
