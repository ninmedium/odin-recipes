Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a hearty, layered dish that combines tender sheets of pasta with a rich, meaty tomato sauce, creamy ricotta, and melted mozzarella. Each bite is a perfect balance of savory flavors, with hints of garlic, basil, and oregano elevating the comforting layers. Baked to golden perfection, it's the ultimate crowd-pleaser for any gathering.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna noodles
  • Ground beef or Italian sausage
  • Ricotta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook lasagna noodles until al dente and brown the ground beef or Italian sausage with garlic and tomato sauce.
  2. Layer the cooked noodles, meat sauce, and ricotta cheese in a baking dish, repeating the layers and topping with mozzarella.
  3. Bake at 375°F (190°C) for 30-40 minutes until bubbly and golden, letting it rest before serving.
