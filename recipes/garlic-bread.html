Garlic Bread

Description

Golden and crispy on the outside, yet soft and buttery on the inside, garlic bread is the perfect sidekick to any meal. Each slice is infused with the rich, savory flavor of garlic, complemented by a hint of fresh parsley and melted cheese for a satisfying finish. It’s the ultimate comfort food, balancing crunchy and tender textures in every bite.

Ingredients

  • Fresh garlic cloves, minced
  • Unsalted butter, softened
  • Baguette or crusty bread

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C) and slice the baguette in half lengthwise.
  2. Mix softened butter with minced garlic, then spread generously over the cut sides of the bread.
  3. Bake for 10-12 minutes until the bread is golden and crispy, then garnish with fresh parsley if desired.
